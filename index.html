Because we care about instruments.

We’ve seen some fabulous and unique instruments during our 150 years. We pride ourselves in our careful selection of the finest in every price range. Then, where necessary, we restore them to tip-top condition. Finally our technicians and luthiers dedicate time to set them up, so you can experience their full potential for yourself in our store.

Because practice makes perfect.

To help you achieve your goals in music we have an unrivalled selection of Printed Music as well as all the necessary accessories for your instrument. Select from the range yourself or contact us - we can help you to identify the piece of music, string or reed that is just right for you or your budding Marr, Elton, Brendel or Callas.
